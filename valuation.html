VALUATION SERVICES

We have a dedicated team of professionals with various technical backgrounds approved by THE INSTITUTION OF VALUERS (IOV) & THE INDIAN INSTITUTION OF VALUERS (INDIA) for handling valuation of Plant & Machinery and Immovable Assets. We provide valuation services to estimate true worth of assets for individuals/Financial Institutions and Banks. Valuation Report may be required for the following purposes.

  1. Valuation for Company Accounts and Financial Statement
  2. Valuation for Receivership/Insolvency
  3. Valuation for Merger, Acquisition and Takeover
  4. Insurance Valuation
  5. Market Value of Plant & Machinery
  6. Forming Part of the Continuing Business
  7. To remain “in-situ”
  8. Valuation for Removal from Premises
  9. Valuation for Visa Purpose
  10. Valuation for Repair/Renovation Estimation& Housing Loans
  11. Property Valuation for Capital Gain
  12. Scrap Valuation
  13. Income Tax Valuation
  14. Wealth Tax Valuation
