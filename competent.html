Competent Person Certification-Delhi

Cogs Associates has been authorised by Chief Inspector of Factories, Labour Department, Government of NCT of Delhi, under certificate of competency dated 15.05.2015 as “Competent Person” under following sections of the Factories Act in the state of Delhi for inspection and testing of equipments by competent person.

  1. Section 21 (2) – Dangerous machines
  2. Structure Stability
  3. Section 29 – Lifting Machinery, Chains, Ropes and Lifting Tackles
  4. Section 31 – Pressure Plants
