About US

About Our Services

Insurance Survey & Loss Assessment

We have IRDA licensed surveyors to carry out professional work of Survey & Loss Assessment of General Insurance Industry claims in the field of Fire, Marine Cargo and Engineering by experienced surveyors and loss assessors. We also provide ancillary services like Investigations, Claim Consultation, Full Policy Review, Legal Consulting, Portfolio Analysis, Risk Inspection and Route Survey

Valuation

We have a dedicated team of professionals with various technical backgrounds approved by THE INSTITUTION OF VALUERS (IOV) & THE INDIAN INSTITUTION OF VALUERS (INDIA) for handling valuation of Plant & Machinery, Computer Technology, Electronics Telecom, Instrumentation and Immovable Assets (Civil). We provide valuation services to estimate true worth of assets for individuals/Financial Institutions and Banks.

Chartered Engineer Services

As an Independent Chartered Engineer (Civil, Mechanical and Electronics & Telecom) by THE INSTITUTION OF ENGINEERS (INDIA), we are authorized for issuing certificates required by Customs, Banks and Financial Institutions for various aspects. We have experienced Chartered Structural Engineer for assessment of structure stability..

Competent Person Services

We have been authorised by Chief Inspector of Factories, Labour Department, Government of NCT of Delhi and Rajasthan under certificate of competency as “Competent Person” for following sections of the Factories Act for inspection and testing of equipments by competent person for Section 21 (2) – Dangerous machines, Section 28 – Lifts and Hoists, Section 29 – Lifting Machinery, Chains, Ropes and Lifting Tackles and Section 31 – Pressure Plants.

Engineering Services

We at Cogs Group provide following engineering services by energetic team of experienced professionals to meet the requirement of our clients. Energy Audit, Datacenter Benchmarking, Third Party Inspection, Risk Inspection, Quantity Survey, Stock Audit, Pre Shipment Inspection, TEV Study, Asset Componentization and Identification, Listing & Preparation of Fixed Asset Register.

Asset Componentisation

We are well known service provider having technical expertise for Identification of assets, Grouping of assets for separate locations/ categories & Break up of assets based on the elements have materially different estimated useful lives for “component approach” in accounting for property, plant and equipment. This is not only useful in complying with Companies Act 2013 and IFRS requirements but also helps to improve the fixed assets register and make depreciation accounting more reflective.
